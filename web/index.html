How to play ▾

Be the first to collect 3 Trios — or flip the 7 on your turn for an instant win.

1 Setup

  • Cards numbered 1–12, three of each (36 total).
  • Everyone gets a hidden hand. Leftover cards form the center pile face-down.

2 On your turn

  • Reveal a card from any of these sources:
    • any center pile card,
    • any opponent's lowest or highest card,
    • your own lowest or highest card.
  • Keep revealing more cards from the same sources — they all have to be the same number as your first reveal.
  • Three matching cards = you score a Trio and they leave the game.
  • Reveal a card that doesn't match and your turn ends — every revealed card returns face-down.

3 Winning

  • Collect 3 Trios to win the game.
  • Or flip the 7 from the center on your turn — instant win!
